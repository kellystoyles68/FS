About Me

Where I come from:

I was born on a rock on the "Rock" in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean : Bell Island, Newfoundland. In 1996, I went back to school at the age of 26 and got my college diploma in Computer Studies - Management Information Studies.

But life had other plans for me and over the next 25 years, I got married, raised 2 children, went into the financial sector as an independant Insurance Broker. During those years, I also did part-time/contract work for a Software Engineering firm.

During the pandemic, I sold my business and went to work at ICANMarine. It was during my time there, I got to dabbled in almost every department but one - the software engineering side. I discovered, I yearned to create. I wanted to see something i created come to life! So almost 30 years later, I became a student again - this time at Keyin College in the Software Development program.



Where I am going:

I don’t know yet and that’s what makes this so exciting!!

With two semesters completed, I will graduate at the end of December 2024. In the new year, I could be anywhere but I do know, I will either be developing some awesome code or flashy websites. Watch out world, here I come!! That’s a little about me; I’d love to hear from you!  I’m always open to speak more about my experiences, portfolio pieces, and new design opportunities.
